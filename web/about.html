Ye Dang Korean Language Center





Founded in 2023, Yedang Korean Language Center in Myanmar is dedicated to inspiring local students by teaching not only the Korean language but also its vibrant culture. Our mission is to help students who dream of a future in Korea by providing them with the skills and understanding they need to thrive in both the language and the rich cultural landscape of Korea. At Yedang, we empower the next generation to turn their Korean dreams into reality.




Unlock opportunities in Korea
  • Korean Language Courses: Learn basic Korean, with classes focused on K-pop and culture.
  • Full Support for University Admission: Complete guidance to help you enter Korean universities.
  • Work Opportunities: Gain part-time jobs at partner companies while you study.
  • Financial Benefits: Earn higher wages in Korea compared to Myanmar.
  • Achieve Your "Korean Dream": Study, work, and build a brighter future in Korea.
